## Extrinsic evaluation
Extrinsic evaluation tests word embeddings on external tasks like named [[NER]], [[POS tagging|POS tagging]], etc.

- + Evaluates actual usefulness of embeddings
- - Time consuming
- - More difficult to trouble shoot
